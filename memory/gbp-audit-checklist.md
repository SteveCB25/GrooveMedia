# Google Business Profile Audit Checklist
**Groove Media — Client Onboarding Tool**  
*Complete this for every new client during setup*

---

## SECTION 1: Profile Completeness (One-Time Setup)

### Business Info
- [ ] Business name matches exactly what's on their signage/license (no keyword stuffing)
- [ ] Primary category is correct (e.g., "HVAC Contractor" not just "Contractor")
- [ ] Secondary categories added (up to 9 — e.g., "Air Conditioning Contractor", "Furnace Repair Service")
- [ ] Business description written (750 chars max) — includes top 3 services + city/region
- [ ] Phone number is local (not an 800 number)
- [ ] Website URL linked
- [ ] Service area set correctly (cities/ZIP codes they actually serve)
- [ ] Hours of operation complete including "by appointment" if applicable
- [ ] "Special hours" set for holidays

### Services
- [ ] All services listed individually (not just "HVAC" — list "AC Installation", "Furnace Repair", "Duct Cleaning", etc.)
- [ ] Each service has a description and price range if applicable

### Photos
- [ ] Minimum 10 photos uploaded
- [ ] Cover photo is professional (team, truck, or completed job — NOT a logo)
- [ ] Profile/logo photo uploaded
- [ ] Photos include: team, equipment/truck, completed jobs, before/after if possible
- [ ] Photos are geotagged (take photos on-site with location on)

## SECTION 3: Citation Consistency Check

Check that business name, address, and phone (NAP) match exactly on:
- [ ] Google Business Profile
- [ ] Yelp
- [ ] Facebook Business Page
- [ ] HomeAdvisor / Angi
- [ ] BBB (Better Business Bureau)
- [ ] Thumbtack (if applicable)
- [ ] Apple Maps
- [ ] Bing Places

**Flag any inconsistencies** — even small differences (St. vs Street, LLC vs no LLC) hurt rankings.
