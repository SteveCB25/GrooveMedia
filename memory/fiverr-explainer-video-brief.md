## PROJECT TITLE
60-Second Spokesperson Explainer Video — AI Lead System for Contractors

---

## ABOUT THE PRODUCT
Groove Media (thegroovemedia.com) is an AI-powered lead generation system built specifically for home service contractors in Maryland — HVAC, plumbing, roofing, electrical, landscaping, and general contractors. The system puts an AI phone assistant on a contractor's business line that answers every call 24/7, captures caller information, and automatically sends the contractor a text with the lead details. It also books appointments and sends follow-up messages — all without the contractor lifting a finger.

---

## TARGET AUDIENCE
Small to mid-size home service contractors. Typically male, 30–55 years old, always on the job site, skeptical of technology but motivated by money and not missing jobs. They respond to plain talk, real ROI, and simplicity. Do NOT use jargon like "CRM," "automation," or "pipeline." Speak their language.

---

## TONE
Confident, direct, conversational. Think: a trusted business advisor talking to a tradesman — not a tech salesperson. Warm but no-nonsense. Avoid corporate language.

---

## SCRIPT (60 seconds)

> "If you're a contractor, you already know — when you're on the job, you can't always answer your phone. And when you miss that call, you miss the job.
>
> Groove Media fixes that. We put an AI assistant on your business line that answers every call, day or night — even on weekends. It asks the caller what they need, gets their contact information, and sends you a text with everything you need to follow up.
>
> No app to learn. No system to manage. Your leads just show up as texts.
>
> And if a customer speaks Spanish? No problem — the assistant handles that too.
>
> Here's the math: one extra job a month from a call you would have missed — that pays for the whole system.
>
> Groove Media. Never miss a lead again.
>
> Visit thegroovemedia.com to get started today."

---

## ON-SCREEN DIRECTION
- "answers every call" → show a phone ringing / being answered
- "sends you a text" → show a text message notification on a phone
- "no app to learn" → keep it clean, no cluttered UI shots
- End card: **thegroovemedia.com** with tagline **"Never Miss a Lead Again"**

If no B-roll: clean neutral background (white, dark grey, or light blue). Avoid anything too flashy — the audience is blue-collar.
